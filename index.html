How to screenshot each tile
1. Tap any tile above to open it full screen
2. For image tiles: tap the tile to select your photo from camera roll
3. Take a screenshot (power + volume up on iPhone)
4. Crop to the square in Photos app
5. Upload to Instagram from your camera roll
For panoramic pairs (02+03, 07+08) select the same wide photo in both tiles. Upload the right tile first, then the left — this creates the seamless 2-tile spread in your grid.
